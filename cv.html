Sound Artist // Developer // Interaction Designer
Born in Bologna (IT), currently resident in Graz (AT), I'm active in the fields of music, visuals and software development. My research spans several aspects of sound design, human-computer interaction, multi-channel technologies and interactive installations. I have a keen interest in design and realization of electroacoustic devices, multi-platform software controllers/tools and production of sounds using anything from programming languages to welding machines.

Some of my works were presented at Sound and Music Computing Conference(SMC2025), Elevate Festival 2025, Fusion Festival 2025, Sound and Music Computing Conference(SMC2021), New York City Electroacoustic Music Festival (NYCEMF 2021) and others
